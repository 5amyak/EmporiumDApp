Welcome to Emporium

Online marketing and retail stores but the most powerful source of influence comes from the buyer's family and peer groups. Emporium, a web app which deals with the above issue by targeting social influencers from sites that can potentially influence customers using existing popular social sites like Facebook, Twitter and Quora etc. Users select a particular post in the Emporium site related to their area of expertise to post on a social networking site and are rewarded according to the number of likes received and users who liked and share will also be awarded. Emporium deals with utilizing the power of small scale social influencers to increase the sales by using a bottom up rather than top down approach. The transactions are committed using the blockchain technology for the purpose of more transparency, decentralization, stable and secure transfers.
